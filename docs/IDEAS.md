# FIRST DAWN IDEAS

This document is a parking place for ideas that may become research tasks later.

* Event replay for understanding how major historical moments emerged.
* Family tree views that make ancestry feel meaningful without turning people into statistics.
* Cultural artifacts that can begin as memories and become traditions, myths, or laws.
* Region-specific discovery paths so different societies can diverge naturally.
* Observer summaries that describe what happened without flattening individual lives.
* Explicit production, staging, and sandbox world selection in every admin tool.
