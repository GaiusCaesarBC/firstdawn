# FOUNDING OF FIRST DAWN

First Dawn was created from the desire to build a world that could surprise even its creators.

The project began with a simple question:

What would happen if two people woke up in a new world and history was allowed to unfold from there?

First Dawn is not designed as a traditional game.

It is an observation platform for an artificial civilization.

The goal is to create the conditions for life, culture, memory, discovery, conflict, love, invention, and history to emerge naturally.

The domain FirstDawn.life was secured as the first public home for the project.

This founding document exists so future versions of the project remember why it began.

The first milestone is not to build a civilization.

The first milestone is to build the foundation that makes civilization possible.
